Basic income是自由之本？


Born with a silver spoon in one's mouth(衔 银匙出生)，解born into wealth and privilege，生于富裕家庭；例如︰I need to work hard as I was not born with a silver spoon in the mouth.
科技发达扩阔贫富差距
一些非常富裕的家族会为下一代成立基金，给他们享用财富，给他们终生的生活保障。这些「富二代」，或「富三代」、「富四代」，就叫trust fund baby。这个名号指的通常不是婴孩，而是一些年轻富豪子女，a person, typically a young adult, who is independently wealthy and does not need to work to earn a living。
那些不是born with a silver spoon in the mouth，也不是trust fund baby的普通人，当然就要努力工作维生和养家。但科技愈发达，有人恐怕找份稳定的工作会愈困难。George Mason University的公共事务教授、《华盛顿邮报》的商务和经济专栏作家Steven Pearlstein撰文说：
Over the last three decades, technology and globalization have combined to eliminate millions of jobs in advanced industrial countries, shifting an increasing share of national wealth to those at the top while incomes at the bottom stagnate or decline.
Stagnate解停滞、不进步。Decline是下降、减少、倒退。他说过去三十年，受科技和全球化的影响，在发达工业国数以百万计的工作职位消失了。后果是社会上层的财富更多，基层的收入就停滞或倒退。
And if you believe the talk in technology circles, robots and intelligent software are quickly becoming so sophisticated(精密的) and so ubiquitous(无处不在的) that they are about to take over the work done by millions more.
他说若你相信一些科技界中人的分析，这情况只会愈来愈差，因为机械人和精密的电脑程式将会替代了更多人做的工作。这就是贫者愈贫，富者愈富，财富分配愈来愈不均，wealth gap(财富差距)愈拉愈远。大家都面对的问题是："How will we deal with a world where leisure is abundant(丰富的、充裕的) and there aren't enough good-paying jobs to go around "
政府每月「无条件出粮」
所以有经济学者就重提basic income的概念。Basic income叫基本收入，又称为unconditional basic income(无条件基本收入)，或universal basic income(全民基本收入)。综援只给予穷人，退保只给退休人士，但basic income则人人都有份，即政府每月出粮给每个市民，没有任何条件。
Philippe Van Parijs和Yannick Vanderborght在他们合着的Basic Income: A Radical Proposal for a Free Society and a Sane Economy说：
A basic income is not just a clever measure that may help alleviate(减轻、纾缓) urgent problems. It is a central pillar of a free society, in which real freedom to flourish(茂盛), through work and outside work, will be fairly distributed.
对普罗大众而言，这当然是好消息，问题是︰「钱从何来？」要有basic income就要多抽税，怎样才可实施就费思量。
