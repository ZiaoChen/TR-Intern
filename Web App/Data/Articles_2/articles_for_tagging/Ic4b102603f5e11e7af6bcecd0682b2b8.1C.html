幸運便士日


主持︰Judd Boaz
Today is Lucky Penny Day. It could also be your lucky day if you find a penny on the ground. "See a penny, pick it up. All day long you'll have good luck." Superstitious people only pick up pennies lying face up.
今天是幸運便士日，提醒我們地上拾到「公」向上的硬幣，會帶來幸運。我們都知道a coin has two sides(凡事都有兩面)，可不要can't make heads or tails(完全搞不明白)或penny wise, pound foolish(撿了芝麻，丟了西瓜)。有時候，我們也須in for a penny, in for a pound(一不做，二不休)。
